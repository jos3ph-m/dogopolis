The Curious Beagle

The Beagle was originally bred as a scent hound and their noses are still used by many hunters today. Clever and energetic, this breed needs lots of activity to stay happy and healthy. Their hound blood gives them a loyal and tenacious character. Sturdy and solid, Beagles are considered “big” for their small size.
