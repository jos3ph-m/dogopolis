The beautiful Pug

The pug is a breed of dog with physically distinctive features of a wrinkly, short-muzzled face, and curled tail. ... Pugs are known for being sociable and gentle companion dogs. The American Kennel Club describes the breed's personality as "even-tempered and charming".
